I am a reporter, copy editor and fact-checker available for freelance or hire. As a reporter, I have experience covering government and various local news and have worked in a wide range of styles, from breaking news to longform investigative journalism. My fact-checking work includes several longform stories for the Boston Globe Magazine.

I graduated summa cum laude from Tufts University in May 2020 with a B.A. in political science and international relations. I served as managing editor of the school's independent student newspaper, the Tufts Daily, in Fall 2018 and later as its executive investigative editor. While at Tufts, I developed an interest in political reporting during internships in the U.S. Senate and the Massachusetts State Senate.
